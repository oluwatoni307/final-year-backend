Our Process

Six guiding principles that shape our solutions

1

Vision

This is the foundation of our design process. It involves defining the overall goals and objectives for the project. We consider the bigger picture and set a clear direction to guide our work.

2

Research

We conduct thorough research to gather valuable insights. This includes studying relevant data, trends, and technologies that can inform our design decisions. Research helps us stay up-to-date and ensures that our solutions are innovative and effective.

3

User & Market Analysis

Understanding the needs and preferences of our target audience is crucial. We analyse user behaviours, demographics, and pain points. Additionally, we examine the market landscape to identify opportunities and challenges.

4

Ideation

In this creative phase, we brainstorm and generate a wide range of ideas. We encourage innovative thinking and explore multiple design concepts. This step is about thinking outside the box and finding unique solutions.

5

Prototype / Testing

We bring our ideas to life through prototypes — from sketches to interactive models. Prototyping allows us to test and refine designs iteratively, gathering feedback from users and stakeholders for continual improvement.

6

Validation

In the final stage, we validate our designs to ensure they meet the project's goals. This involves usability testing and performance checks to make sure solutions are functional and ready for implementation.

Our Mission

At the heart of our company is a mission to constantly develop and deliver life changing solutions.

Our Vision

To make life fun and easier for everyday people. We are committed to making this vision a reality by focusing on creating products and services that simplify daily tasks for people.

Since our founding, we have dedicated ourselves to making a positive impact through thoughtful and purposeful design. Our journey is just beginning, and we are excited to continue growing and evolving with you. Together, we can achieve great things and make life a little easier for everyone.

Our Design Philosophy

At Ojutu, we embraced a design philosophy known as BASED, which stands for Beautiful, Accessible, Safe, Effective, and Durable. We believe that for any solution to be successful, it must embody these principles.
